## 📌 Overview

This repository presents a **model-free Online Disturbance Learning Vector Control (ODL-VC)** framework for **bidirectional Voltage Source Converters (VSCs)** in hybrid AC/DC microgrids.

The proposed controller enhances classical vector control by embedding an **online adaptive disturbance estimator**, enabling:

- Real-time compensation of unmodeled dynamics  
- Robust DC-link voltage regulation  
- Stable power exchange under dynamic and uncertain conditions  
- No offline training or explicit system modeling

## ⚙️ Key Features

- ✅ Hybrid AC/DC microgrid simulation (Python-based)
- ✅ 24-hour load and generation profiles (compressed to 24 s)
- ✅ Bidirectional power flow (import/export operation)
- ✅ Tight DC-link voltage constraint band (±4 V around 680 V)
- ✅ Model-free disturbance learning (lightweight ANN)
- ✅ Constraint-aware adaptive attenuation mechanism
- ✅ Robustness analysis:
  - Sampling frequency variation (10 kHz vs 15 kHz)
  - ±30% parameter uncertainty in \( L_f \), \( R_f \)

---

## 📊 Simulation Highlights

- 🔹 DC-link voltage deviation reduced by ~40–50% vs PI control  
- 🔹 Faster transient recovery (≈1.2 s → <0.6 s)  
- 🔹 Stable operation under unbalanced and dynamic loads  
- 🔹 Low sensitivity to parameter mismatch and sampling variation
